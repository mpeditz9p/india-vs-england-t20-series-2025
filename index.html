India vs England T20 Series 2025

Match Results and Details

Match 1: India vs England

Date: January 22, 2025

Venue: Eden Gardens, Kolkata

Result: India won by 7 wickets (with 43 balls remaining)

Top Performers:

  • India: Abhishek Sharma – 79 runs off 34 balls
  • England: Jos Buttler – 68 runs off 44 balls

Summary: India chased down a target of 133 runs in just 12.5 overs, with Abhishek Sharma's explosive batting leading the way. England's innings were restricted by India's bowlers, with Arshdeep Singh and Varun Chakravarthy taking crucial wickets.

Match 2: India vs England

Date: January 25, 2025

Venue: M.A. Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai

Result: India won by 2 wickets

Top Performers:

  • India: Tilak Varma – 72* runs off 55 balls
  • England: Brydon Carse – 31 runs off 17 balls, 3 wickets

Summary: In a thrilling encounter, India chased down 166 runs with four balls to spare. Tilak Varma's unbeaten 72 guided India to victory, while Brydon Carse's all-round performance kept England in the game.

Match 3: India vs England

Date: January 28, 2025

Venue: Rajkot

Result: England won by 26 runs

Top Performers:

  • England: Ben Duckett – 51 runs off 28 balls, Liam Livingstone – 43 runs off 24 balls, Adil Rashid – 1 wicket for 15 runs
  • India: Varun Chakravarthy – 5 wickets for 24 runs, Hardik Pandya – 40 runs

Summary: England posted 171/9, with key contributions from Ben Duckett and Liam Livingstone. Varun Chakravarthy's 5-wicket haul gave India a chance, but despite Hardik Pandya's fightback, India fell short, finishing at 145/9.

Match 4: India vs England

Date: January 31, 2025

Venue: Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium, Pune

Time: 7:00 PM IST

Result: India won by 2 wickets

Top Performers:

  • India: Hardik Pandya – 52 runs off 40 balls
  • England: Ben Duckett – 34 runs off 24 balls, Sam Curran – 3 wickets for 35 runs

Summary: India chased down 165 runs with just one ball remaining, led by Hardik Pandya's fighting 52 runs. Sam Curran's three-wicket haul was crucial in pushing the game to the final stages.

Match 5: India vs England

Date: February 2, 2025

Venue: Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai

Time: 7:00 PM IST

Details: The final match of the 5-match series. India leads 3-1, and England will try to secure a win and make a comeback in the series.
